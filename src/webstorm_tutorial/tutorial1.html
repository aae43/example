    Step 1

    Create new project

    Step 2

    Make sure the project is in HTML5 Boilerplate. Name the project and make sure it's in the most updated version.

    Step 3

    Go to tools on the top of the screen and click on "Start SSH session"

    Step 4

    Put in your user name and password. For the host type in "afs12.njit.edu" and click OK.

    Step 5

    After it connects you to afs12. On the bottom next to the terminal, make sure you are on the right screen. Type "pwd" to see where you are. You wanna be in the public html so this is how you get to it. type "cd public_html" and then type "pwd" again. Copy the line that is generated because we will need that soon.

    Step 6

    Go to tools at the top of the screen and hover over "depolyment" and then click on "configuration"

    Step 7

    Click on "SFTP" and then hit "OK".

    Step 8

    Type in "AFS" and then hit "OK".

    Step 9

    Type in the same host you used to connect to the SSH session. Put in your user name and passord. And then paste the line we copied in step 6. For Web Server UR: tpye in http://web.njit.edu/~(your username/ucid).

    Step 10

    Click on Mappings at the top of the page. On the line that says "Local path" type the name of your project all the way at the end. Copy that name and paste in the 2 lines under as well. Then hit "OK". If you do not see the remote host box at the right hand side of your screen, search for it by clicking the search button and tpying in "Remote Host".

    Step 11

    Click on tools and hover over "Deployment" and click Automatic Upload.

    Step 12

    Right click on your project name at the top of the screen and hover over "Deployment" and click "Upload to AFS". Then you're set to go and anywork you do will automatically be upload to AFS.
